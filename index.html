Mastermind: Crack the Code

Instructions

  1. Guess a combination of colors by clicking on the colors in the order you want your guess.
  2. The game will fill in empty peg slots with a black peg for the amount of colors you guessed correct color and correct location and a white peg for amount of colors you guessed correct color but wrong location.
  3. Using logic, determine what to guess next and make your next several guesses (up to 9) to figure out what the secret code of colors is.
  4. If you guess the secret code in 9 moves or less, you win! If not, you lose.
